Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

VilleCabs ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our ride-hailing platform at villecabs.com or through our mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and phone number when you register
  • Location data: Your GPS location during ride booking and while a ride is active, to connect you with nearby drivers and provide live tracking
  • Ride information: Pickup and drop-off locations, fare amounts, ride history, and ratings
  • Device information: Browser type, device type, and IP address for security purposes
  • Driver documents: Drivers submit identification documents (licence, fitness certificate, registration) for verification
  • Communications: Messages sent between drivers and customers through the in-app chat

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect passengers with available drivers in Manchester, Jamaica
  • To calculate fares based on distance and route
  • To provide real-time ride tracking for passenger safety
  • To verify driver identities and vehicle documents
  • To send ride confirmations and updates
  • To resolve disputes and investigate safety incidents
  • To improve our platform and services

3. Location Data

We collect your location when you book a ride and during active rides. Driver location is shared with the assigned passenger in real time for safety and tracking purposes. We do not track your location when you are not using the app or do not have an active ride.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Drivers: Your first name and pickup location are shared with your assigned driver
  • Passengers: Driver name, vehicle details and license plate are shared for safety
  • Firebase (Google): Our platform uses Google Firebase for authentication, database, and storage
  • Law enforcement: If required by law or to protect the safety of our users

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), Firebase Authentication, and Firestore security rules to protect your data. Driver documents are stored securely in Firebase Storage with restricted access.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Ride history is retained for record-keeping and dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking (note: this will affect app functionality)

8. Children's Privacy

VilleCabs is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email. Continued use of VilleCabs after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
